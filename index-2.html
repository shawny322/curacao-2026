What to Pack

Beach Essentials (All): Swimwear, cover-up/rash guard, reef-safe sunscreen, sunglasses, hat, sandals/water shoes, beach tote, quick-dry towel.

For Women

  • 2–3 swimsuits + cover-up
  • Flowy dresses / skirts
  • Cute day outfits for photos
  • Heels or wedges for night
  • Statement jewelry + clutch
  • All-White Night: White dress/fit + neutral shoes
  • Light sweater for breezy nights
  • Makeup setting spray + mini perfume

For Men

  • 2–3 swim trunks + rash guard
  • Linen/cotton shirts (short-sleeve)
  • Chino shorts + breathable tees
  • Loafers or clean sneakers for night
  • Watch + sunglasses (polarized)
  • All-White Night: White button-up + white/neutral pants & shoes
  • Light jacket for evening breeze
  • Grooming kit + deodorant wipes

Extras (All): Portable charger, waterproof phone pouch, reusable water bottle, travel-size toiletries, basic meds, copies of IDs.
